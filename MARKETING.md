# MusicScan AI - Marketing Assets

## 📱 App Description (Play Store)

**Short Description:**
AI-powered music library scanner that analyzes your songs for mood, genre, and energy — then creates smart playlists instantly.

**Full Description:**
Transform how you experience your music library with AI-powered analysis and smart organization.

🔍 **Smart Scanning**
- Automatically scans and catalogs your entire music library
- Extracts metadata: title, artist, album, duration, genre

🧠 **AI-Powered Analysis**
- Mood detection (energetic, chill, melancholic, euphoric, aggressive, romantic)
- Genre classification using Gemini AI
- Duplicate song detection

✨ **Smart Playlists**
- Generate AI playlists from text prompts like "chill workout music" or "road trip vibes"
- Organize by mood, genre, energy level, or custom rules

📊 **Beautiful Insights**
- Visual breakdown of your library by mood and genre
- Track listening statistics and favorites

🎵 **Seamless Playback**
- Built-in audio player with queue management
- Shuffle, favorites, and quick actions

**Perfect for:** Music lovers who want their library organized and personalized without manual work.
